# Install Attendance App on iPhone

## Step 1: Start Public Server on Your PC

Double-click: `START_PUBLIC_SERVER_CLOUDFLARE.bat`

Wait for the URL to appear. It will look like:
```
https://random-name.trycloudflare.com
```

**Copy this URL!**

---

## Step 2: Install on iPhone

1. **Open Safari** on your iPhone (not Chrome!)
2. **Paste the URL** from Step 1
3. Wait for the app to load
4. Tap the **Share button** (square with arrow at bottom)
5. Scroll down and tap **"Add to Home Screen"**
6. Tap **"Add"** in the top right

---

## Step 3: Use the App

- Find "Attendance" on your Home Screen
- Tap to open - it runs fullscreen like a native app!
- Works offline after first load

---

## Notes

- Server must be running on your PC for the app to work
- The URL changes each time you restart (unless you use Cloudflare account)
- Works on any device on any network!
